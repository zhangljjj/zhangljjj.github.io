Research Interest

 I work in the field of UAV autolanding, pose estimation, computer vision and deep learning theory. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Combined data-driven with deep learning detection strategy for UAV landing vision task
  • Responsible for the construction of UAV sequence image dataset for approaching and landing.
  • UAV online location estimation using cascaded lightweight deep learning model
  • Research on the rapidity and accuracy of image mosaic algorithm
  • Mainly engaged in image perception and deep learning research
